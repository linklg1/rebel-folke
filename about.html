Meet the Artists (and their work)



Shae Segagkind

Shae Seqaqkind is an Irish and Lenape woman living on unceded & stolen xʷməθkʷəy̓əm, Sḵwx̱wú7mesh, and sƏl̓ílwƏtaʔɬ lands. She is a practicing artist, art therapist, registered psychotherapist (qualifying), and art educator. Shae is passionate about fostering creativity in people of all ages and thinks that creativity is the closest thing that we have to magic. Shae hopes that the work she creates brings joy to people’s spaces. Follow Shae on Instagram
